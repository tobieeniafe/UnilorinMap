How The Map Works

Dashboard

This shows few of the major locations on thr University campus and other places of interest.

Find Me

This makes use of your geo-location services with your consent, and ses GPS for accuracy. Due to the absence of GPS on desktop devices, locations may not be 100% accurate on desktop mode.

About Map

This helps with the navigation around the campus. Taking 3 inputs from you, which include: From (your current location), To (your proposed destination) & Mode (driving or walking depending on your preference).
The dotted blue and gray lines represent walking/footpaths (which could be motorable in some cases), while the solid blue lines represents driving routes.
